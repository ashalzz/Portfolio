Side Scroller Scratch Game - "Starfish Adventure":

I recently created a side scroller game called Starfish Adventure using Scratch with Kaylen. The player is the starfish and its controls are similar to that of the flappy bird bird (use spacebar to jump, falls naturally). The fish, sharks, and seashells come at different times and paces, but are coded very similarly. The player's goal is to play as long as possible and get as many seashells as they can. If you touch a shark, you die immediately, but for each fish you touch, you lose a seashell. Once you hit a negative seashell count, you die. This is the first game I ever coded on Scratch or in general.
